About MoodApp

MoodApp lives on your wrist, and lets you record your mood throughout the day. You can get it for free on the Pebble Appstore.

Features include

- Configurable reminders
- 1,7, and 30-day averages
- 1,7, and 30-day historical bar graphs on your wrist
- Location tracking
- Sync to cloud (coming soon)
